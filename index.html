Google Calendar embed URL
In Google Calendar, go to Settings → your calendar → Integrate calendar, then copy the URL from the "Embed code" iframe src attribute. It looks like:
https://calendar.google.com/calendar/embed?src=you%40gmail.com&ctz=America%2FNew_York

To show multiple calendars, add more &src=... parameters to the URL.
